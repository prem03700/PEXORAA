FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before we start building together.

Still have questions?

Our team responds within 24 hours. Reach us at pexxoraa@gmail.com or use the contact form.

What makes PEXXORAA different from other agencies?
We combine design artistry, engineering precision, and AI expertise under one roof. Unlike traditional agencies, we're builders first — every team member codes, designs, or does both. We don't outsource, and we don't pad timelines.
How long does a typical project take?
Simple websites: 7–14 days. Complex web apps or AI integrations: 4–10 weeks. We give you a detailed timeline during discovery and we hold to it. Rush delivery is available for most projects.
What do you need from me to get started?
Just your vision. Fill out our contact form, and we'll schedule a free strategy call. After that, we handle the heavy lifting — research, planning, design, development, and launch.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance?
Yes. Every project includes 30 days of post-launch support. For ongoing needs, we offer monthly retainer plans that cover updates, security patches, performance optimization, and feature additions.
What technologies do you work with?
React, Next.js, Node.js,Html&css, Python, TypeScript, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, AWS, Vercel, Supabase, LangChain, and more. We use the best tool for the job — not just the most popular one.
How is pricing structured?
Fixed-price projects for well-defined scopes. Time-and-materials for evolving products. Retainers for ongoing work. All pricing is transparent and discussed upfront — no hidden costs or surprise invoices.
